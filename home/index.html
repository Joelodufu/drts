THE PARTWAY

Jurney to become a licensed driver

You can start the jurney of becoming a licensed driver through the following partways. or you can chose to byepass the other processes so long as you have been registered

1. Registered Driving Schools

Explore a list of registered driving schools in your area. Choose a trusted school to kickstart your journey towards becoming a safe and responsible driver.

2. Driver's License Application

Apply for your driver's license with ease through our online application process. Follow the simple steps to get on the road legally and safely.

3. Booking Driving Tests:

Schedule your driving test on your terms. Choose your preferred date, time, and location, and embark on the path to becoming a licensed driver.

4. Renewal of Driver's Licenses

Don't let your license expire. Renew it hassle-free and continue enjoying the freedom and responsibilities that come with being a licensed driver.
